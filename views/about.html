My name is Katie Colford, and I am a student in the architecture major at Yale University, concentrating in design. Outside the studio, I give tours at the Yale University Art Gallery and tours of campus, and I have worked in the Conservation Department at the Yale Center for British Art. I am the co-head of the undergraduate architecture society Ink & Vellum and have contributed artwork to Yale publications including the Yale Literary Magazine. I am fascinated by architecture as the intersection of art, philosophy, literature, science, and technology, and I am especially interested in the theoretical underpinning of the built environment.
